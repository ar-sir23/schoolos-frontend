How it works

Built around the school day, not the other way round

No training manual required — if a teacher can use a phone, they can use this.

Morning

Mark attendance once

Each class teacher marks attendance in under a minute. Parents see it the same day, not at the end of term.

Mid-month

Fees track themselves

Every payment — cash, bKash, bank — lands in one ledger. No more chasing a paper receipt book.
